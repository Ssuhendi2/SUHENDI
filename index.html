About Me

As a seasoned Oil & Gas field professional with a career spanning since 2005, I bring nearly two decades of hands-on expertise in critical upstream operations. My specialization lies in Surface Well Testing, Data Acquisition (DAQ), Multiphase Flow Meter Vx (MPFM) multi rate test, and Slickline Operations.

I am driven by a commitment to operational excellence, data integrity, and upholding the highest standards of safety (HSE). Throughout my career, I have successfully managed complex field projects, ensured accurate production data, and optimized well performance.
